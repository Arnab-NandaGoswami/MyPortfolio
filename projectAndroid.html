IdeaBook

Technology Used :-
Java, XML, Android Studio, Firebase

I've made this wonderful app named IdeaBook for those users who really desires for a platform to share their thoughts effortlessly and fluently without any hagitation to others. Here like a facebook or any other social media site, users will get a common platform to share their thoughts through text and images. You will also get a private chatting option to chat with your close ones You can appriciate the thoughts of the others by hitting a like to other's posts and also can comment their posts for raising your voice and your thoughts towards other's thoughts. You can also see and manage your own post differently. You can edit your posts but can't do anything with other's posts. You can also set up your profiles and set your name, e-mail, phone number. You can also set your DP and profile picture and also chage them whenever you need.
